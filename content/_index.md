## About me

I am a physicist, fascinated by finding patterns. You can see this fascination in the questions I have chased: why bacteria form biofilms, how ant trails emerge, when public opinion reaches consensus (for more, my publications are [here](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=vjDY4AgAAAAJ&hl=en&oi=ao).) Or in my data science work: [pattern discovery in music](https://github.com/zmokhtari89/k_means_klang), where machine learning clusters sound beyond genre, or [spotting a specific object](https://github.com/zmokhtari89/roof_detection) in a messy, real-world image.

But what captures my joy is neither physical laws nor programming alone. It's solving problems. Always. And doing it well, with everything I have: math, statistics, ML, AI, and I keep coming back for more.
